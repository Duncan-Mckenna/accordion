A note on accessibility: instead of toggling the aria-expanded state for the accordions, I have instead made the contents of the accordions redable without interaction. This is closer to the paradime of the experience of a screen reader as they would scan through headers to find relevant paragraphs, not minding wheather an element they can't see is open or closed.
